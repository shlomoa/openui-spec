# Charts

Charts are visual representations of data, including bar charts, line charts, pie charts, and similar visual summaries.

## Scope

A chart definition may describe:

- chart identity,
- chart type,
- data series,
- axes or labels,
- legends,
- tooltips or details,
- empty state,
- loading or error state, and
- accessible textual alternatives.

The specification does not require a specific charting library, canvas, SVG, HTML, or framework component implementation.

# Lists

Lists are ordered or unordered collections of items with optional sorting, filtering, and pagination.

## Scope

A list definition may describe:

- list identity,
- item identity,
- item labels or content,
- sorting behavior,
- filtering behavior,
- pagination or incremental loading,
- selection behavior,
- empty state, and
- loading or error state.

The specification does not require a specific list component, virtual-scroll strategy, data source, or framework component.

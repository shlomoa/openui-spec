# Date/Time pickers

Date/Time pickers are calendar-based date and time selection components.

## Scope

A date/time picker definition may describe:

- picker identity,
- date selection behavior,
- time selection behavior,
- allowed range,
- disabled dates or times,
- display format,
- parsing expectations,
- validation behavior, and
- accessible labels or descriptions.

The specification does not require a specific calendar widget, locale library, time-zone library, input mask, or framework component.

# Dialog

A Dialog is a modal or non-modal interaction surface with a title, content, and actions.

## Scope

A dialog definition may describe:

- dialog identity,
- title,
- content region,
- actions,
- modal or non-modal behavior,
- open and close behavior,
- focus management expectations, and
- accessible labels or descriptions.

The specification does not require a particular dialog component, overlay service, portal mechanism, or framework-specific accessibility implementation.

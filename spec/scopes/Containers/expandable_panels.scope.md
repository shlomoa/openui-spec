# Expandable panels

Expandable panels are containers that can be expanded or collapsed to show or hide content.

## Scope

An expandable panel definition may describe:

- panel identity,
- heading or summary,
- content region,
- expanded or collapsed state,
- default state,
- toggle behavior,
- grouping behavior, and
- accessible state exposure.

The specification does not require a specific accordion, disclosure widget, animation system, or framework component.

# Forms

Forms are read-write data views that let users enter, edit, validate, and submit business data.

## Scope

A form definition may describe:

- form identity,
- fields,
- labels,
- input constraints,
- validation rules,
- submission behavior,
- dirty state,
- reset or cancel behavior, and
- loading or error state.

The specification does not require a specific form library, data-binding model, validation engine, transport protocol, or framework component.
